Our Story: From Hills to Hand

Kigali Kickstart Coffee was born from a passion for the rich, complex flavors found in Rwandan highlands. We partner directly with small-holder farmers, ensuring fair wages and sustainable practices that protect the land and empower local communities.

Our beans are hand-picked, wet-processed to perfection, and roasted locally in Kigali to bring out the distinct notes of citrus, black tea, and brown sugar unique to this region. It's more than coffee‚Äîit's a commitment to excellence and community.
